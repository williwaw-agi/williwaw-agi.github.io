Williwaw*

Williwaw will you take you on a virtual adventure, on a sailing boat across the sea. Get on board and control the elements in order to sail where you have never sailed before. Williwaw allows a number of adventurers to control the virtual environment simultaneously, using multiple mobile phone devices. Blow into your phone to create the wind in the sails, make noise to shape the waves and tilt your phone to steer the ship's wheel.

*insert video here.
Williwaw is an application developed by four students at KTH Stockholm, as part of the course Advanced Graphics and Interaction, class of 2019.

* Williwaw: "A sudden blast of wind descending from a mountainous coast to the sea."
